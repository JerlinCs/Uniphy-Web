FAQs

Answers to all your Uniphy pricing questions

What counts as a billable resource?

Billable resources include any infrastructure or workload managed via Uniphy.

Cloud Instances & Workloads: AWS EC2, RDS, ELB; Azure VMs, SQL DBs, Load Balancers.
Containers & Services: Each deployed container or application instance counts as a resource.

Whatâ€™s the difference between Free and Standard?

Free Plan: Includes infrastructure provisioning and monitoring.
Standard Plan: Adds automated fixes, advanced scaling, CI/CD integration, and deployment pipelines.

Can I connect multiple infrastructure sources?

Yes! All plans allow unlimited cloud accounts, clusters, and workloads.

Can I invite unlimited teammates?

Yes! Uniphy supports unlimited users on all plans.

Do I need a credit card to try Uniphy?

No. You can start using Uniphy immediately without a credit card.

What if I manage more resources than my plan includes?

Free Plan: Usage is limited to your included resources. Additional resources require upgrading.
Standard Plan: Automatically scales to cover additional resources, billed at the end of the cycle.

How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Upgrade from Free to Standard anytime via your account dashboard. For downgrades or Premium inquiries, contact our team.

Do you support open-source projects?

Yes! Uniphy offers sponsored resources for free, publicly accessible projects. Reach out to learn more.

Existing Uniphy customers: You can expand your plan, enable additional features, or scale resources anytime. Contact your account manager for assistance.
